## Runtime / network use in Tools

The **Tools → Balance** panel may issue optional HTTP lookups against public blockchain explorers for address balances. That traffic is operator-initiated and separate from third-party cracker licenses.

## Honesty

GUI labels that mention Hashcat, John, or BTCRecover are bridges to the tools above — not reimplementations of their attack engines. Always comply with each project's license if you redistribute a bundle that includes their binaries.
